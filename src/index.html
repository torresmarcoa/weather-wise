What is WeatherWise?

Welcome to WeatherWise! This app makes checking the weather simple and reliable. Whether you're planning your day, preparing for a trip, or just curious about different places, WeatherWise provides real-time, accurate details like temperature, wind speed, pressure, and more. Plus, you can quickly revisit your last three searches for easy access.

How to Use WeatherWise

  1. Search for a Location: Enter a city name in the search bar and select (click) on a suggestion.
  2. Check the Weather Details: View temperature, wind speed, pressure, and more in real-time.
  3. Switch Between Units: Click on the unit buttons to change between Celsius, Kelvin, and Fahrenheit.
  4. Review Your Search History: Your last three searches will be saved for easy access.
  5. Close the Weather Window: Click the "X" button in the top right corner.
  6. Dark Mode Option: Toggle dark mode for a comfortable viewing experience.
